## 1.1.0 - 2017-01-30
* Compatible with PHP 5.6 or higher.

## 1.0.0 - 2017-01-30
* Compatible only with PHP 7.0 or higher. In the next versions, the library will be modified to make it compatible with PHP 5.6 or higher.
